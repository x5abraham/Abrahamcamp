    • Canadian Natural Wonders: Explore Wild Canada!

    So you’ve had a life changing summer working at a fun filled Canadian summer camp. Now it’s time to take off and explore the rest of beautiful country. Whether you want to explore nature, have a wild adventure or just see some cool creatures, Canada has it all and more. Here’s some Canadian natural wonders we think you need to see to complete the summer of a lifetime!

    Aurora Borealis

    It’s guaranteed you will never see anything quite as beautiful as the Aurora Borealis. Also known as the Northern Lights, this mystical phenomenon has had people from all corners of the globe travelling to see it for themselves. In Canada, they are lucky enough to have several view points of the Lights in the Sky, such as British Columbia and Albera. Surely it’s one for the bucket list.
